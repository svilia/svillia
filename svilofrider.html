SVILOFRIDER

Open World Driving Experience

Discover

A Living City. Endless Roads.

Svilofrider is an open-world driving game developed entirely with HTML5 Canvas technology. Players can freely explore a detailed city filled with highways, residential districts, industrial areas, intersections, traffic lights, parking lots and continuously expanding environments. The project focuses on realistic driving, optimized performance and a natural atmosphere instead of exaggerated visual effects. Every update expands the world with new vehicles, missions, artificial intelligence improvements, weather systems, day-night cycles and customization features.
