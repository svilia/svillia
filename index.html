Studio

Independent. Focused. Built for Driving.

Svilia Games Studio is an independent game studio focused on creating immersive driving experiences with HTML5 Canvas technologies.

Our goal is not just to create another driving game, but to build a living open world that grows with every update.

We believe clean design, smooth gameplay and attention to detail create better games than unnecessary visual effects.

Features

Designed Around Driving

Open World

Explore highways, neighborhoods, industrial zones, parking areas and intersections without loading screens.

Living Traffic

Cars obey traffic lights, use lanes correctly and create a believable city atmosphere.

Optimized Performance

Built with HTML5 Canvas for smooth gameplay even on mid-range hardware.

Future Updates

New districts, vehicles, missions, weather and AI improvements are continuously planned.

Vehicle Physics

Responsive steering, satisfying acceleration and natural handling are at the core of the experience.
